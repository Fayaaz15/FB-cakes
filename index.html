Our Story

Baked with Love,

FB Cakes – Fresh Bakes was born from a single belief: everyone deserves an extraordinary cake. What started as one store in Chennai has grown into Tamil Nadu's most cherished bakery chain, with over 50 outlets and a million smiles delivered.

We bake fresh every single day, using only the finest ingredients — no preservatives, no shortcuts. Whether it's a child's first birthday, a golden anniversary, or a grand wedding, we pour the same passion into every single cake.
